The Technology

At MATA, we are at the forefront of agricultural innovation. Our dedicated team is developing an advanced autonomous machine specifically designed to perform efficient and precise thinning operations on fruit trees. Powered by cutting-edge technologies, we are revolutionizing fruit production and transforming farming practices.

Our autonomous machine utilizes computer vision and artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms to analyze fruit trees with exceptional accuracy. Through intelligent analysis, it can identify mature and underdeveloped fruits, selectively targeting and removing excess fruits to optimize the final crop yield.

A remarkable feature of our technology is the implementation of a unique decision-making algorithm. This algorithm combines real-time data from sensors, including laser-based measurement systems, with criteria set by farmers and market demands. The lasers are precisely used to remove selected fruits that need to be thinned, ensuring that the remaining fruits can grow to their optimal size.

The use of laser beams in our machine enables precise and efficient fruit removal. Calibrated for accuracy, the lasers delicately remove the targeted fruits while minimizing damage to trees and surrounding fruits. This cutting-edge technology, combined with our machine's autonomy, significantly reduces labor requirements and increases overall productivity.

With our advanced technology, farmers can achieve consistent and high-quality fruit yields while optimizing resource allocation and reducing waste. Our solution empowers farmers to meet market demands effectively, providing fruits that align with size preferences and ensuring profitability.

Today

Today, the manual thinning process in agriculture is labor-intensive and time-consuming. On average, a worker can only finish less than 0.5 acre per day, resulting in limited productivity and increased costs.

However, with the revolutionary MATA robot, the game changes. Our autonomous machine has the capability to efficiently manage 5-10 acres per day, significantly increasing productivity and saving valuable time for farmers.

Not only does the MATA robot outperform manual labor in terms of speed, but it also ensures a higher percentage of fruits are in the final desired size. Through advanced computer vision and AI algorithms, the robot can precisely identify and remove excess fruits, allowing the remaining fruits to grow to their optimal size. This leads to improved crop quality, higher yields, and reduced waste.

By adopting the MATA robot, farmers can streamline their operations, increase productivity, and achieve greater profitability. Embracing cutting-edge technology in agriculture is the key to transforming traditional practices and ensuring a sustainable future for the industry.
